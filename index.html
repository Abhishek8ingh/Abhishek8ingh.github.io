About Me

Hi, I’m Abhishek Singh, a computer science student from India. I’m passionate about solving real-world problems—especially the ones that no one else is working on—using technology, AI, and innovation.

For me, building things from the basics and taking them all the way to the top level is what I truly enjoy. I love understanding concepts from their deep roots to their highest applications.

Beyond technology, I’m deeply fascinated by space and its mysteries. Learning about space gives me a sense of calmness, almost like meditation. In my free time, I enjoy exploring the latest developments in technology and space—especially from the private sector.

I also like sharing my thoughts and ideas on social media, particularly on X (formerly Twitter).
